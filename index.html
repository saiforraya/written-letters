what if..

February 5, 2026

“I catch myself everyday thinking about you

I don’t get why, it’s been months and I still think about you; I still check your accounts your TikTok reposts before you changed usernames or before you blocked me, I still care.

Because what if

I did things differently? I thought differently?
What if I tried harder?

My life has been full of regrets, I think and think and think and I regret regret and regret lots, I let go of my regrets but the only regret I couldn’t let go of was when I ruined our relationship

I only have myself to blame.

You played a big role in my life

And you affected me, not in a single bad way, all I have of you is good, all I remember is your smile, your laugh, the times you’d giggle, the times you’d watch while on call with me, the times I’d admire you doing your own thing while I’m there admiring you

You came into my life for a reason

What was the reason? I don’t know.
So why?”

birthday

February 5, 2026

It was my birthday today, yet the only thing I could think about since the moment it turned 12, was you; I would be lying if I said I didn’t wait for a response, I want to hear back from you

Every hour of the day I check sendthesong, instagram just incase it didn’t notify me of a message from you yet all I see is, “liked a message - 16hrs ago.”

I saw a note in sendthesong, it had white Ferrari on it. I couldn’t help but wonder if it’s you, I don’t wanna reach out for the third time and ask in case it isn’t you

And what if, you never wanted to hear from me again? What if the like reaction meant me to stop? Then that would seem I’d be pushing. I don’t want to push

So here I am, writing hoping you’d come back and check

Anyway, I had a great birthday it was simple but I was surrounded by my close family even when I requested that I didn’t want any celebration, mwuahaha the ice cream was yummy!

Deep inside I wish I could’ve spent this birthday with you, and all my future ones.

the waves that reminded me of you

hi rayaa, i don’t know why i’m writing this when i know it won’t reach you.

maybe it’s the sound of the waves. being here feels nostalgic—back when i used to wish you were here with me so we could experience it together, take morning strolls here together.

i don’t know why you keep showing up in my mind in every place i used to go when we were still together. maybe it’s because i always wished you were there with me whenever i visited new places, whenever i saw something beautiful. i can’t help but imagine your smile seeing this view… and then i end up admiring you more than the view itself.

watch the waves

the waves are pretty, aren’t they? even the whole ride on the way here, you were on my mind. i kept wishing i could’ve brought you with me.

Unconditional

you were, and still are, my image of unconditional love. the seven months we had weren’t perfect, but you stayed—loving me even when it was hard for you, even when i didn’t treat you the way you deserved, even when i was immature.

it wasn’t the best for me either, but deep down i knew you were the embodiment of unconditional love. i hate that it took me so long to realize that. i hate that i took you for granted while you were there, loving me in every way you could.

and now i want to reciprocate what you gave me. i’d do anything just to give back even a portion of the love you once gave me. i know the love i gave you back then might not have been the kind of love you imagined or needed, and i know i’m too late. i ruined the one chance you gave me to make it right, and it kills me.

The Plumeria

I’m not sure if you remember, but do you remember when I took a picture of a plumeria flower? And I told you, “while the other flowers were starting to rot, you were there perfect as everrr hihihi.” Mwuahahaha.

I still see you the same way. Like that flower—still perfect in my eyes. Maybe you see me as one of the plumerias that wilted, and maybe I did. But I want you to know I didn’t pick that flower randomly. I looked through every single plumeria there just to find the most perfect one, because that’s the one that matched how I saw you. The others were wilting, and I might’ve wilted too… but you were still there, blooming as ever.
